With 3D Touch, Live Photos, 7000 series aluminum, A9 chip, advanced cameras and so much more; you’ll see how with iPhone 6s the only thing that’s changed is everything.


New 3D Touch Technology.

In addition to familiar Multi‑Touch gestures like Tap, Swipe, and Pinch, 3D Touch introduces Peek and Pop. This new 3D touch lets you preview all kinds of content without having to actually open it. For example, lightly press the display to peek at each email in your inbox. Then when you want to open one, press a little deeper to Pop into it.

A better, seamless design.

The iPhone 6s is made from a new alloy of 7000 series aluminum. The 4.7-inch and 5.5-inch Retina HD displays are constructed with the strongest cover glass used in any smartphone. And a distinctive new rose gold finish joins the existing lineup of space gray, silver, and gold.

The new iOS

The most intuitive, advanced, and secure mobile operating system in the world gets major enhancements: built-in apps, faster performance, convenient updates, a longer battery life, a smarter Siri and search, and much more.
